# Operations

## Cadences

- Monthly global PM team syncs (all verticals)
- Auto Staking: reconvene Mondays to review models before governance
- Marketing/product announcements post-IPO: may require outside counsel re-approval; team to clarify which initiatives require review + typical timelines

## Governance / Review Requirements

- Auto Staking: requires RMCC approval + likely OC (Operations Committee) review; avoid "illusion of extending credit"; opt-in feature
- OC charter: allows non-fiduciary actions; clients can leave qualified custody for permissionless pools; enables institutional DeFi (Maple, etc.)
- AUC receipt tokens: plan to count as AUC (1:1 redeemable IOU via legal agreements)

## Asset Onboarding Process

- Automation-driven; strategic chain list prioritization
- Cost target: <$10k/chain (down from $25k)
- Volume target: 40 chains/quarter (up from 6–7)
- EVM sandboxing: two batches complete; 8 chains shortlisted from Top-1000 EVM list; commercial alignment required
- ChiliZ only commercially aligned so far ($150K)

## Staking Ops (Auto Staking)

- Beta: ~7 clients (2 each from Strategic, Tier 1, Tier 2); GA one cohort while pre-communicating to other; give CSMs heads-up
- Exchange onboarding: later (regulatory/UX constraints: withdrawal SLAs; reward/principal co-mingling; ledger reconciliation)
- Liquidity management: define withdrawal SLA + reliability target; per-asset liquidity stack; automation policy for buffer + triggers + route selection
- Insurance/slashing: working with Jody + external insurance partners (Figment comps: balance sheet + on-chain cover via Nexus Mutual + AON catastrophic)
- M&A: Emerick to propose/quantify M&A targets (buy now vs kill and acquire later for liquid staking)

## Figure Markets Ops

- All economic actions initiate in Figure UI (buy/redeem yields, buy/sell tokenized equity, request USD wire); settle to BitGo custodial wallet
- Nothing initiated from BitGo UI
- OFAC + sender/receiver matching checks on wires
- Phase 1 reporting via Figure; BitGo reports may lag
- Recovery: Figure as registered digital transfer agent can deprecate/remint tokens if wallet compromised; tokens restricted from leaving Provenance

## Token Management Ops

- Grantee communications: whitelisting for Tools for Humanity and others
- Telemetry: grant wallet → Go Accounts → trade/stake (by end of month)
- On-chain OTC: exploring synchronized unlock days with on-chain price codification

## Compliance / Legal Flags

- Privacy coins: likely hard-no for shielded coins unless auditors/regulators/OC have transparency access
- Binance: concerns about flat 10% ownership—push for utilization/volume alignment
