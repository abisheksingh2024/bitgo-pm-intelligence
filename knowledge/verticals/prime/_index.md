## What This Vertical Owns

Prime covers BitGo's institutional brokerage, lending, and trading services. It serves hedge funds, family offices, and large institutional clients who need custody + liquidity + settlement in an integrated stack. The Go Network is the liquidity and lending layer that underpins Prime.

**Core products:**
- Go Network (lend/borrow, AUC flywheel)
- OTC trading and settlement
- Prime brokerage services
- Institutional lending
- Collateral management
- Client reporting and portfolio views
