> **Stub** — Awaiting PM brain file contribution. See `framework/templates/vertical.md` for the template.

## What This Vertical Owns

SCaaS enables financial institutions, fintechs, and payment companies to issue and manage their own stablecoins using BitGo's compliant, battle-tested infrastructure. This covers the full stablecoin lifecycle: minting, burning, reserve management, compliance reporting, and multi-chain deployment.

**Core products:**
- Stablecoin issuance platform (white-label)
- Reserve custody and attestation
- Minting / burning workflows
- Multi-chain deployment (EVM + non-EVM)
- Compliance reporting and transparency tools
- Central Bank Digital Currency (CBDC) infrastructure (exploratory)
