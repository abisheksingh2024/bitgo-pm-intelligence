# Canton Network (Digital Asset)

## Status Summary

BitGo is an active Super Validator (SV) on Canton. Chain-level wallet support is **live** as of Q4 2025. Token support (USDCX + CBTC) was targeted for **end of February 2026** but is still **in progress** as of 2026-03-02 — confirm actual delivery date with Abhishek.

## Key Facts

- **Wallet support go-live**: Nov 2025 (deposits 11/5, withdrawals 11/11, 1-step deposits 11/14)
- **Token support target**: USDCX + CBTC by end of Feb 2026 — *not yet confirmed delivered*
- **SV weight**: Weight-5 approved (CIP-0074)
- **Cantoncoin rewards**: ~$1.16/Cantoncoin; ~$28M/yr at Year 3 (70% treasury lock)
- **SV milestone vesting**: custody enablement → Canton token standard → Canton-native asset custody → client adoption
- **Current AUC**: ~$459M Canton volume today → ~$1B target in 6 months
- **Long-term horizon**: 1–3 yr for tokenized US Treasuries + DTCC settlement; ~5% of tx fees + SV rewards
