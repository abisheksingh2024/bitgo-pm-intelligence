# New PM Onboarding Checklist

Welcome to BitGo Product! This checklist is your guide for the first 30 days.

---

## Week 1 — Orientation

### Day 1–2: Product Stack
- [ ] Read `knowledge/shared/product-stack.md` (full BitGo product map)
- [ ] Read `knowledge/shared/glossary.md` (key terms and acronyms)
- [ ] Ask Claude: "Give me an onboarding walkthrough of [your vertical]"

### Day 3–4: Your Vertical
- [ ] Read `knowledge/verticals/[your-vertical]/_index.md`
- [ ] Read your vertical's `team.md`, `roadmap.md`, `deals.md`
- [ ] Read your PM owner's brain file: `pms/[owner-name].md`

### Day 5: Company Context
- [ ] Read `knowledge/shared/okrs.md` — understand all vertical OKRs
- [ ] Read `knowledge/shared/org-chart.md` — who owns what across Product

---

## Week 2 — People & Processes

- [ ] Schedule 1:1s with each member of your team
- [ ] Meet your key cross-functional partners (Legal/OC, Sales, Trust Ops, Engineering)
- [ ] Attend your vertical's recurring syncs (standup, planning, review)
- [ ] Get added to relevant Slack channels

---

## Week 3 — Go Deeper

- [ ] Read all partner records in `knowledge/verticals/[your-vertical]/partners/`
- [ ] Read all topic deep-dives in `knowledge/verticals/[your-vertical]/topics/`
- [ ] Ask Claude: "What are the biggest risks and open items in [your vertical]?"
- [ ] Set up your connector integrations (`config.yaml`)

---

## Week 4 — Own It

- [ ] Create your own brain file: copy `framework/templates/brain.md` to `pms/your-name.md`
- [ ] Fill in your current initiatives, OKRs, and context dump
- [ ] Add yourself to `pms/_index.md`
- [ ] Run your first `pm-debrief` after a meeting
- [ ] Commit and push your brain file to the repo

---

## Key Questions to Ask Claude

```
"Give me a full onboarding overview of the Ecosystem vertical"
"What is BitGo's staking product and how does it make money?"
"Explain HSM and how BitGo uses it"
"What is Canton Network and why does BitGo care about it?"
"Who are the key partners in [vertical] and what's their status?"
"What are this quarter's company-wide OKRs?"
"What should I know before my first meeting with [partner/team]?"
```

## Glossary Quick Reference

See `knowledge/shared/glossary.md` for the full glossary.

Most common terms for new joiners:

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| AUC | Assets Under Custody |
| NRE | Non-Recurring Engineering (one-time integration fee) |
| OC | Operations Committee (internal governance) |
| RMCC | Risk Management and Compliance Committee |
| MPC | Multi-Party Computation (key management technology) |
| HSM | Hardware Security Module |
| SCaaS | Stablecoin as a Service |
| CaaS | Crypto as a Service |
| SV | Super Validator (Canton Network) |
| LST | Liquid Staking Token |
| OES | Off-Exchange Settlement |
| Go Accounts | BitGo's integrated yield/utilization account product |
